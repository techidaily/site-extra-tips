**Meta Description:** People love to add stickers to TikTok videos to make them attractive, but they can also be distracting. Learn how to remove stickers from a TikTok video in this article.

One trend that doesn't seem to be going away is the use of stickers on TikTok videos. Today, most people add stickers and emojis to their videos on TikTok to make the video exciting and engaging. However, sometimes, you come across a video with stickers on it, and you want to enjoy the video without the stickers getting in the way.
